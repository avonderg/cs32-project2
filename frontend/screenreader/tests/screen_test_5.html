All About Tapirs

Tapirs describe a family of mammals with iconic looking snouts. They are odd-toed ungulates, like horses and rhinos, which means that they have hooves but also an odd number of toes. There are 4 extant species of tapirs: Barid's, South American, Mountain, and Malayan.
